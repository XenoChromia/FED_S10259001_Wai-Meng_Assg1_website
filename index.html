ADOMINATION

Ado (アド) is a popular Japanese utaite, born on October 24, 2002, in Tokyo, Japan. She first gained attention as a YouTube singer before making her major debut. Ado's breakthrough came with her 2020 single "Usseewa," which became a viral sensation and cemented her place in the music industry. Known for her powerful vocals, Ado captures the angst and hopes of Japan’s younger generation through her music. She has worked with prominent figures in the industry, including lending her voice to the soundtrack of the anime film ONE PIECE FILM RED (2022), where her song "New Genesis" topped the Billboard Japan Hot 100 chart for several weeks. Ado's style blends rock, pop, and anime music, making her a versatile and influential artist. In addition to her success with singles like "Show" (2023) and "Kura Kura" (featured as the opening theme for Spy × Family), Ado continues to release impactful music, showcasing her unique voice and stage presence
